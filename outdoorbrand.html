Outdoor Brand Website

Objective

This project involved working with a team of web developers to design an e-commerce website for Outdoorbrand, an outdoorswear camping/hiking company. The team was tasked with designing a site and incorporating creative ways to engage users and display/sell products. The team did this by creating interactive “Trail Cardsˮ that allowed users to click on interactive cards to show information (such as conditions and suggested equipment) about outdoor trails.

Process

The first step was deciding as a team what we wanted the website to look like and what we needed it to do. Next we worked together to create low fidelity wireframes of the website and prototypes o f functional aspects such as the trail cards. Then we worked with feedback from the stakeholders to improve upon our design and continued to create mid/high fidelity designs in Figma. Once the team and stakeholders were happy with the design and prototypes, we submitted the final deliverable of the website.

Results

This project was completed successfully on time and within budget. Our team collaborated closely to refine design concepts and develop engaging, user-friendly interfaces. You can view the full mockup on Behance
